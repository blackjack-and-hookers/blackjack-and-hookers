**Welcome to Blackjack-and-Hookers** — the GitHub org that exists because two developers looked at their already-unmanageable pile of repos and said, *"You know what this needs? A collaborative layer of chaos."*

**Contributor A** brings the raw, untamed energy of a mass that has figured out how to open a terminal. Adorable. Terrifying. Will `force push` to main and look you dead in the eyes while doing it. No remorse. Only commits.

**h-e-d-o-n** brings... *gestures vaguely at a mass grave of 47 active projects, an engineering methodology with its own versioning system, and a config directory deeper than most people's existential crises.* Look, someone had to be the one with "a process."
